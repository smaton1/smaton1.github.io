About Me:

Hello there! I have recently graduated from General Assembly after a three month course in back and front end web development. I picked up a number of new skills over the duration of the course including the use of ruby and rails, javascript, jquery, angular and the use of postgres databases. This course built upon my existing enthusiasm for front and back end web development and fuelled my desire to learn more. Combined with my University experience my time at General Assembly built upon my existing communication skills and my ability to work well in a team. I still believe that I have a lot to learn but I now have a solid base to work from as well as the work ethic and the deep rooted interest in web development to allow me to forge a successful career as a web developer.

Other Interests:

I have played basketball for around ten years now and it is something that I am very passionate about. I enjoy being part of a team and have played for a number of teams including the Cambs county team, two different school teams and the Lincoln University mens 1st team. I currently play for a local Cambridge team, and watch it on a regular basis. I have made life long friends on all of these teams and enjoy socialising with all of them.

I am also a massive video games fan. I follow a number of competitive teams across a number of e-sports including Dota 2 and Hearthstone! When I am not playing video games or basketball I enjoy socialising with friends and enjoy good beer, the Cambridge beer festival being one of my favourite times of the year.
